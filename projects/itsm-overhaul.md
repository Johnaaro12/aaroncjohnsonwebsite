# ITSM Overhaul

**Project Type:** Enterprise Redesign · **Scale:** 5,000+ seats

---

## Overview

Led a full redesign of ITSM processes across Incident, Problem, and Change Management modules for a large enterprise environment.

## What I Built

- Automated assignment rules based on CI, category, and location
- SLA tracking with breach notifications and escalation paths
- Custom dashboards for leadership visibility into backlog and resolution metrics
- Standardized change request workflows with risk-based approval routing

## Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Avg Resolution Time | 18 hours | 12 hours (-34%) |
| SLA Compliance | 72% | 94% |
| Self-Service Adoption | 15% | 48% |

## Technical Details

- Migrated all legacy workflows to Flow Designer
- Implemented Assignment Data Lookup rules for auto-routing
- Built Performance Analytics dashboards with threshold alerts
- Created ATF test suites for all critical workflows

## Key Takeaways

> The biggest win wasn't technical — it was getting stakeholders aligned on process before touching configuration. We spent 3 weeks in process design workshops before writing a single business rule.
